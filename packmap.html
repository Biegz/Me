PackMap | Full Stack App Development

With more and more people getting into the niche of multi-day hiking and backpacking, many are left wondering what gear is the 'right' gear to bring with them on the trail. PackMap aims to help the everday hiking enthusiast by giving them the tools they need to stay organized when planning for a hike.

Task

This project was started in the summer of 2021. Challenges so far have included: collection of branded gear data and database design, API structure and development, and UI/UX Design. The beta release of this application is expected by February, 2022.
