Speedy Spot | Full Stack App Development

Speedy Spot is an Android and iOS app that helps alleviate parking congestion at Stony Brook University. It is a crowdsourcing application, by which faculty, and students can input their current parking location. With enough users, we are able to estimate the availability of each lot and how occupied a particular lot may be.
